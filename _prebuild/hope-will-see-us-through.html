Typography & Music

Hope Will See Us Through is a side project I started early this year. I was listening to a song, Its Not A Bad Little War by Bayside, for what must have been the thousandth time. At some point in the breakdown, I realized that I had listened to it over and over, but I had never actually heard it.

It was at this moment that I chose the very next line and decided to create a typographic blog dedicated to bringing lyrics to life. And so, HOPE was born.

Process

I originally intended to create a new piece every week. This plan quickly changed because I realized that just picking a line in a song and making a piece out of it wasn’t what I really wanted.

I wanted to take music that meant something to me and really feel it. Take a line that could send shudders down your spine and make it immortal.

This is why my updates have become erratic. I won’t update just for the sake of updating. I’ll update when a song strikes me and I will listen to that song for hours while I sketch. At some point, lyrics work their way into the sketches and thats when they make their way onto the screen.
